ABOUT GNITS
------------
G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology and Science (GNITS) is a premier women's engineering college located in Shaikpet, Hyderabad, Telangana. Established in 1997, it is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH) and approved by AICTE. The college is NAAC accredited and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

B.TECH ADMISSION PROCEDURE
----------------------------
GNITS B.Tech admissions are done through:

1. EAMCET (Engineering Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test) - Telangana State
   - Conducted by TSCHE (Telangana State Council of Higher Education)
   - Based on rank in TS EAMCET exam
   - 70% seats (Category A) filled through EAMCET counseling
   
2. Management Quota (Category B)
   - 30% seats filled by college management
   - Based on merit in qualifying examination and interview
   
3. NRI Quota
   - For Non-Resident Indian students
   - Apply directly to college with required documents

CATEGORY A SEATS (EAMCET):
Step 1: Appear for TS EAMCET (usually in May)
Step 2: Download hall ticket and rank card from TS EAMCET website
Step 3: Wait for counseling schedule published by TSCHE
Step 4: Register for web counseling on tsche.ac.in
Step 5: Pay counseling fee (Rs. 1200 for OC, Rs. 600 for BC/SC/ST)
Step 6: Exercise web options (preferred colleges and branches)
Step 7: Seat allotment announced based on rank, category, and preferences
Step 8: Report to college with documents for admission

CATEGORY B SEATS (MANAGEMENT):
Step 1: Contact GNITS admissions office directly
Step 2: Fill application form (available at college or online)
Step 3: Submit required documents
Step 4: Appear for merit/interview if required
Step 5: Fee payment and confirmation

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
---------------------
B.Tech:
- Must have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry
- Minimum 45% aggregate in PCM (40% for reserved categories)
- Must have valid TS EAMCET rank (for Category A seats)
- Age: 17 years as on December 31 of admission year

M.Tech:
- B.Tech/B.E. in relevant branch from recognized university
- Minimum 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories)
- Valid GATE score preferred (mandatory for JNTUH stipend)
- TS PGECET rank for state quota seats

MBA:
- Any bachelor's degree with minimum 50% aggregate
- Valid ICET (Integrated Common Entrance Test) score for Telangana state seats
- Must have completed 10+2+3 years of education

MCA:
- Bachelor's degree in relevant field with Mathematics as one subject
- Minimum 50% aggregate
- Valid ICET score
