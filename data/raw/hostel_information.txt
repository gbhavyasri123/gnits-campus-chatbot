GNITS HOSTEL INFORMATION
G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology and Science, Hyderabad
================================================================

OVERVIEW
---------
GNITS provides hostel accommodation exclusively for female students (as GNITS is a women's engineering college). The hostel campus is located within the college premises ensuring safety and convenience for students.

- Floors: Ground + 3 floors

HOSTEL FACILITIES
------------------
Room Amenities:
- Cots with mattresses
- Study tables and chairs
- Cupboards with lock facility
- Fans and lights
- Power backup (limited hours)
- Mosquito mesh on windows

Common Facilities:
- Common bathrooms on each floor (Block A)
- Attached bathrooms (select rooms in Block B)
- Hot water facility: 6:00 AM to 8:00 AM and 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM
- Laundry area with washing machines
- Drying area
- Common room with TV and indoor games
- Reading room
- WiFi connectivity (student credentials required)
- CCTV surveillance throughout
- 24-hour security

Mess Facilities:
- In-house mess with hygienic cooking
- South Indian and North Indian menu
- Special Sunday meals
- Doctor-recommended diet options available on request
- Purified drinking water

HOSTEL FEE STRUCTURE
---------------------
Block A - Triple Sharing: Rs. 45,000 per year (including mess)
Block A - Double Sharing: Rs. 55,000 per year (including mess)
Block B - Double Sharing: Rs. 60,000 per year (including mess)
Block B - Single Room: Rs. 75,000 per year (including mess)

Additional Charges:
- Security Deposit: Rs. 5,000 (refundable)
- Hostel Admission Fee: Rs. 1,000 (one time)
- Mess advance: Rs. 5,000 (adjustable against mess bill)

Payment:
- Fee to be paid at the beginning of each academic year
- Online payment available through college ERP portal
- DD in favor of "GNITS Hostel Fund" also accepted

HOSTEL TIMINGS AND RULES
--------------------------
GATE TIMINGS:
- Morning: Gates open at 6:00 AM
- Evening: Main gates close at 9:00 PM
- No entry/exit permitted after 9:00 PM without prior written permission from warden
- Late entry requires written permission from warden and parent/guardian consent

DAILY SCHEDULE:
- Wake up: 6:00 AM
- Morning duties and breakfast: 7:00 AM to 8:30 AM
- College hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- Evening snacks: 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM
- Study hours: 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM (mandatory)
- Dinner: 7:30 PM to 9:00 PM
- Lights out: 10:30 PM

ATTENDANCE:
- Hostel attendance taken twice daily (morning and night)
- Absence without permission will be reported to parents
- Continuous absence for more than 3 days requires medical certificate or parent intimation

LEAVE RULES:
- Day out pass: Students must submit written request to warden by 8:00 AM same day
- Night out/Weekend leave: Written permission from parent/guardian required in advance
- Leave during exam period: Only for medical emergencies with doctor certificate
- Leave application form available at hostel office
- Maximum leaves per semester: 30 days (excluding holidays)

DISCIPLINE RULES:
1. No male visitors inside hostel premises at any time
2. No cooking inside rooms (fire hazard)
3. No use of electric heaters, irons, or other high-power appliances in rooms
4. No loud music after 10:00 PM
5. No ragging of any kind - strict action including expulsion
6. Smoking and consumption of alcohol strictly prohibited
7. Damage to hostel property will result in fine and disciplinary action
8. Mobile phones allowed but restricted during study hours
9. No sub-letting of rooms or accommodating guests without permission
10. Cleanliness of rooms is the responsibility of occupants

VISITOR RULES:
- Female family members can visit between 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM
- Visitors must sign in at the security gate
- Visitors not permitted inside rooms; meeting allowed in visitor lounge
- Male visitors (fathers, brothers) only at college main reception area
- No visitors during exam periods (one week before and during exams)

MEDICAL EMERGENCIES:
- Campus medical center available during college hours
- Night emergency: Contact warden on duty
- Warden has list of nearby hospitals
- Student must immediately inform warden of any health issue
- Ambulance arrangement available through security

HOW TO APPLY FOR HOSTEL
-------------------------
1. Hostel application form available at college admission office or online at gnits.ac.in/hostel
2. Submit completed form with required documents:
   - Recent passport-size photographs (4)
   - Copy of admission letter
   - Parent/guardian ID proof
   - Medical fitness certificate
3. Room allotment based on availability and distance of home from college
4. Priority given to students from other districts/states
5. Pay security deposit and first installment of hostel fee
6. Collect room key and hostel ID card from warden
