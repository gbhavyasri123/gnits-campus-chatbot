MEMBERSHIP RULES
-----------------
Eligibility:
- All enrolled students of GNITS are automatically library members
- Faculty and staff are members for the duration of their employment
- Library card issued at the time of admission

Library Card:
- Students must produce library card for all transactions
- Loss of library card: Report immediately to librarian, Rs.100 for duplicate card
- Library card is non-transferable

BOOK BORROWING PROCESS
-----------------------
Step 1: Search for book using OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) system at library entrance terminals or online at gnits.ac.in/library

Step 2: Note down the book's accession number and shelf location

Step 3: Locate the book from the shelf

Step 4: Present book and library card at the issue counter

Step 5: Librarian will stamp the due date on the book's date slip

Step 6: Return book before due date

BORROWING LIMITS:
- B.Tech Students: 3 books at a time, for 14 days
- M.Tech/MBA/MCA Students: 4 books at a time, for 21 days
- Faculty: 10 books at a time, for 1 semester
- Ph.D. Scholars: 6 books at a time, for 1 month

RENEWAL POLICY:
- Books can be renewed once if no other reservation exists
- Renewal must be done before due date
- Renewal at library counter or online through library portal
- Maximum one renewal per book

RESERVATION POLICY:
- If book is already issued, student can place a reservation
- Reserved books held for 3 days after return
- Student notified via SMS/email when reserved book is available

FINE STRUCTURE
---------------
Late Return Fine:
- B.Tech Students: Rs. 2 per day per book after due date
- M.Tech/MBA/MCA Students: Rs. 2 per day per book after due date
- Faculty: Rs. 5 per day per book after due date

Lost Book:
- Student must report immediately
- Replace with same edition OR pay double the cost of the book
- Minimum replacement cost: Rs. 500

Damaged Book:
- Minor damage: Rs. 50 fine
- Major damage: Replacement cost of book
- Torn pages, writing in book: Rs. 100 + damage assessment

Maximum Fine Accumulation:
- Fine cannot exceed the cost of the book
- Unpaid fines block library access and exam registration
