PLACEMENT PROCESS
------------------
Step 1: Registration
- Students must register with the T&P Cell at the beginning of their final year
- Fill the placement registration form available at the placement cell office or online at gnits.ac.in/placement
- Submit updated resume, recent photograph, and copies of marksheets

Step 2: Pre-Placement Training
- Compulsory aptitude training for all registered students
- Soft skills and communication workshops
- Technical skills enhancement sessions
- Mock interviews and group discussions

Step 3: Company Registration
- Companies visit the GNITS placement portal to register
- Companies submit Job Description (JD) with eligibility criteria
- T&P Cell circulates JD to eligible students

Step 4: Application Process
- Eligible students apply through the placement portal
- T&P Cell compiles shortlisted student profiles
- Profiles shared with recruiting company

Step 5: On-Campus Recruitment
- Aptitude/Written Test
- Technical Interview (1 or 2 rounds)
- HR Interview
- Final selection and offer letter

Step 6: Offer Letter and Joining
- Placement cell verifies and distributes offer letters
- Students must inform placement cell of their joining status

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR PLACEMENT
------------------------------------
1. Minimum 60% aggregate in all semesters (some companies require 65% or 70%)
2. No active backlogs at the time of campus drive
3. Registered with T&P Cell before campus season begins
4. Students who have already accepted an offer from another company may not participate (single placement policy - varies by company)
5. B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and MCA final year students are eligible

2. Technical Training
   - Programming in C, C++, Java, Python
   - Data Structures and Algorithms
   - Database Management (SQL)
   - Operating System concepts
   - Computer Networks basics
   - Duration: 3rd and 4th year

3. Soft Skills Training
   - Communication skills (spoken and written English)
   - Group discussion techniques
   - Presentation skills

4. Mock Interviews
   - Conducted every semester for final year students
   - Industry professionals invited as mock interviewers
   - Feedback provided individually
   - Resume review and improvement sessions

5. Industry Interaction
   - Guest lectures by industry experts
   - Industrial visits to IT companies in Hyderabad
   - Internship guidance sessions

INTERNSHIP INFORMATION
-----------------------
Duration: 2 months (June-July) for B.Tech 3rd year students
Types:
- Company sponsored internships
- Self-arranged internships (require T&P Cell approval)
- Research internships at IITs/NITs (SRIP, IIT summer programs)
- Government internships (DRDO, ISRO, BARC)

Internship Registration:
- Students must inform T&P Cell about internship opportunities
- Company offer letter must be submitted to T&P Cell
- Internship report and certificate mandatory after completion
- Internships may convert to Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs)

Credit for Internship: Mandatory as per JNTUH curriculum (Industrial Training credits)

PLACEMENT PREPARATION GUIDELINES
-----------------------------------
For 1st and 2nd Year Students:
- Focus on CGPA (aim for 7.0+)
- Learn one programming language thoroughly (C/C++/Java/Python)
- Build logical reasoning skills
- No active backlogs

For 3rd Year Students:
- Maintain 60%+ aggregate
- Learn DSA thoroughly
- Practice on LeetCode, CodeChef, HackerRank
- Apply for internships
- Build projects and add to resume
- Prepare aptitude through books like RS Aggarwal, Arun Sharma

For Final Year Students:
- Register with T&P Cell immediately
- Update and polish resume
- Practice mock interviews
- Attend all aptitude and technical training sessions
- Attend company pre-placement talks (PPTs)
- Follow T&P Cell notice board and WhatsApp group
